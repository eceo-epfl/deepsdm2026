Venue

Travel information

The Alpole building of EPFL Valais is located a few minutes away from the Sion train station. There are direct trains connecting Geneva and Lausanne to Sion, and only one connection is required from Zurich. Geneva and Zurich are the closest airports.

Partner hotels

Rooms are pre-booked at preferential prices in these hotels for the DeepSDM day. Please mention you are attending an event at EPFL in Sion when contacting them.

  • Hotel Elite : 15 minute walk to Alpole. Breakfast included. June 11th: single room for CHF 145. June 12th: single/double room for CHF 166/186.
  • Alaïa youth hostel : 5 minute walk to Alpole. No breakfast, but a common kitchen is available. Double room with/without private bathroom: CHF 70/95. Entire dormitory with 4 beds: CHF 120.
  • Moxy hotel : 30 minute walk or a short bus ride to Alpole. Breakfast included. Pre-booked rooms on June 11th: CHF 138 (single occupancy). No rooms available on June 12th.
